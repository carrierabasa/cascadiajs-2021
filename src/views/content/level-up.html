Level Up at CascadiaJS!

CascadiaJS is the premier JavaScript conference in the Pacific Northwest and is being held on Nov 3-4. This year's event will bring together an amazing group of ~750 web developers from across the Pacific Northwest (and beyond).

Over the course of 2 days, you'll hear 20 amazing talks by experts and their fields, covering everything from Web3 to React to leveling-up inside your company.

We will also create lots of opportunities for you to meet people and connect, including scheduling frequent breaks between talks and organizing social events both online and in-person.

Here are just a few of the other amazing things we have in store for CascadiaJS 2021:

A pre-conference Hack Event

Goodie Box with a hoodie, stickers and other fun stuff shipped to you

Opening and Closing virtual parties

In-person hybrid events in Seattle, Portland and Vancouver for vaccinated folks

Opening and Closing in-person After Parties
